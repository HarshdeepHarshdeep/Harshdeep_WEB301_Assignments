Harbour House is located on the beautiful Detroit River at Lakeview Marina right across from the iconic Peche Island. In the location of the legendary Lilly Kazilly's, Harbour House Windsor is a fully updated Hampton Inspired Waterfront Restaurant & Bar. Call about slips - located right in front of our patio. Also providing boat side service for anyone at the marina - merely call us at 226-620-0101 to place your order and we will run it out to you.
