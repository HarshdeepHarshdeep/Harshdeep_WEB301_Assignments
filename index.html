Harbour House Windsor

Harbour House is located on the beautiful Detroit River at Lakeview Marina right across from the iconic Peche Island. In the location of the legendary Lilly Kazilly's, Harbour House Windsor is a fully updated Hampton Inspired Waterfront Restaurant & Bar. Open for Brunch on weekends and daily for lunch, dinner and late-night cocktails. On weekends expect live entertainment during the day on the 4000 sq ft Waterfront Patio or late night inside their 8000 sq ft dining and bar areas. Harbour House also has 5 boat docks reserved and designated for 40ft and 50ft boats/yachts. You can dock your boat at the brand new multi-million-dollar Lakeview Marina. Call about slips - located right in front of our patio. Also providing boat side service for anyone at the marina - merely call us at 226-620-0101 to place your order and we will run it out to you.

Walker's Fine Candies
